What is Claude AI?

  • Claude AI is a generative-AI chatbot and language model developed by Anthropic.
  • It's built on large-language-model (LLM) and “transformer” architecture: given a prompt (text, and in many cases even files or images), it generates what it predicts to be the most appropriate response.
  • Importantly, Claude is designed with a safety/ethics framework. Its responses are guided by a set of principles (sometimes referred to as “constitutional AI”) that aim to reduce harmful, biased or toxic outputs.

Services Offered

Claude AI offers a range of services, including general chatbot functions like writing, editing, and question-answering, as well as specialized enterprise solutions for businesses. Its capabilities include advanced analysis for finance, customer support automation, and connecting to enterprise tools like Jira and Confluence. For developers, there is the Claude Developer Platform with an API for building custom AI agents and workflows.
